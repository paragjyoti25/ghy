WELCOME TO GUWAHATI

Guwahati is the largest city in the Indian state of Assam and also the largest metropolis in northeastern India.A major riverine port city along with hills is one of the fastest growing cities India, Guwahati is situated on the south bank of the Brahmaputra. The ancient cities of Pragjyotishpura and Durjaya (North Guwahati) were the capitals of the ancient state of Kamarupa
